ABOUT

A fuller picture.

I am a Software Engineering student at McGill University — early in the journey, and already building things that would have felt impossible a year ago.

I got serious about AI tools early. Claude Code and Claude Cowork have become genuine parts of how I work — not shortcuts, but amplifiers. They let me move faster, ask better questions, and ship things while still learning the fundamentals underneath them. This portfolio is one example of what that looks like in practice.

At McGill, I am studying the technical disciplines that build real leverage: algorithms, systems, circuit logic, probability. I am also a member of McGill Formula Electric, contributing to the electrical systems of a competitive race car alongside engineers who are genuinely serious about their craft. That environment raises the bar for me every week.

I care about the craft of software — not just that code runs, but that it is clean, intentional, and legible to the next person. HTML and CSS taught me that design decisions are engineering decisions. Building this site taught me more than any tutorial.

I am not the finished product. But I am serious, fast, and only getting started.
